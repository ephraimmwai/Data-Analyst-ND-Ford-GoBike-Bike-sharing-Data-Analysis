Ford GoBike Bike sharing data analysis presentation¶

by Ephraim Mwai¶

Investigation Overview¶

The data presentation is aimed to answer the following questions;

  • Which are the peak hours?
  • How does age groups compare in number of bike rides over time?
  • What time of the week do we have the highest number of rides for customers and subscribers? Is there a difference between customers and subscribers?

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset was gathered from the data source Ford GoBike and merge to a single master data file. The file represents bike rides between stations data from 2017 to 2019(April) for different users across San Francisco, the East Bay and San Jose.

Which are the peak hours?¶

Most frequent trip hours are at 0800hrs and 1700hrs, the time most bike users are commuting to work and back home repectively.

How does age groups compare in number of bike rides over time?¶

The steady and overall increase in bike rides is highly contributed by the age group of 20-30 years. All other age groups tend to have stagnated at around 2000 rides over time.

What time of the week do we have the highest number of rides for customers and subscribers? Is there a difference between customers and subscribers?¶

Most rides are made at around 0800hrs in the morning and at 1700hours in the evening during week days especially for subscribers. Customers use the service mostly on weekends throught the day. This means subscribers simply use the bike rides to commute to work where as customers use the service for leisure.

df_subscribers = df[df['user_type'] == 'Subscriber']
df_customers = df[df['user_type'] == 'Customer']

subscribers_day_time_df = df_subscribers.groupby(['user_type','day', 'time_hour']).agg({'bike_id' : 'count'}).rename(columns={'bike_id':'no_of_rides'}).reset_index()
subscribers_day_time_df = subscribers_day_time_df.pivot_table(index='time_hour', columns='day', values='no_of_rides').astype(int)
customers_day_time_df = df_customers.groupby(['user_type','day', 'time_hour']).agg({'bike_id' : 'count'}).rename(columns={'bike_id':'no_of_rides'}).reset_index()
customers_day_time_df = customers_day_time_df.pivot_table(index='time_hour', columns='day', values='no_of_rides').astype(int)

plt.figure(figsize=(16,10))
plt.subplot(121)
sb.heatmap(subscribers_day_time_df, fmt='d',  cmap='viridis_r', annot_kws={"size": 12},  cbar_kws={'label': 'Number of Rides - Subscribers'})
