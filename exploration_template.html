After merging all the datasets into a single master data file, we have 3254325 rows, and 18 columns in the new dataset. This includes data from 2017 to 2019 April.

Main feature of the dataset include;

  • member_birth_year : Birth year of a user
  • duration_sec : duration
  • start_station_latitude
  • start_station_longitude

The dataset extends through 3 years;2017, 2018 and 2019. With this period we ll be able to analyse the service growth.

Cleaning Tasks¶

  • Missing member birth year

subscribers_day_time_df = subscribers_day_time_df.pivot_table(index='time_hour', columns='day', values='no_of_rides').astype(int)

Subscribers highly use bike rides during weekdays and Customers have a high frequency of rides during the day on weekends.
